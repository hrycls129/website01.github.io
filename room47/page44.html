You swim out into vast emptiness.

The further you go, the more it feels like swimming and not just wading through a pile of plushies. Eventually, you are soaked through. Still, you press on.

Eventually, the infinite horizon is broken by a curved white shore. You approach it, and climb over the ridge. You find yourself tumbling over the edge of a 470 ml soda cup, onto the floor of a fast food establishment.

How, even.
